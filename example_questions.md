# Suggested Insurance Policy Questions

1. What does the policy say about 'unexpected serious illness'?
2. How do I file a claim for hospitalization?
3. Are pre-existing conditions covered?
4. Are accidents during extreme sports covered?
5. Does the policy cover chronic illnesses?
6. What are the exclusions for adventure sports?
7. How long is the waiting period for hospitalization coverage?
8. Are family members included in the coverage automatically?
